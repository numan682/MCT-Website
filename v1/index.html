About Us

Where imagination meets engineering rigor

The Multimedia and Creative Technology Department at Daffodil International University blends storytelling, interaction design, and computing. Since 2010, we have produced creators who build meaningful experiences across web, XR, audio, and motion.

Vision

Lead creative technology education in South Asia through innovation and ethical practice.

Mission

Empower students with cross-disciplinary skills, industry mentorship, and research-driven learning.

Studio Culture

Agile sprints, critiques, and live briefs to mirror creative agencies.

Research

Labs exploring AI art, inclusive typography, sonic identities, and interactive media.

Community

Design jams, film festivals, and hackathons open to all disciplines.

Global

Exchange programs and showcases at SIGGRAPH, SXSW, and Ars Electronica.

Admission Eligibility

Who can apply?
  • General: Minimum GPA 2.5 in SSC & HSC (or equivalent); minimum -C- in English.
  • Diploma: Minimum GPA 2.5 in SSC and 4-year Diploma in Computer Tech/Graphics Design.
  • English Medium: 5 O-level + 2 A-level; among 7 subjects: at least four B grades and three C grades.
